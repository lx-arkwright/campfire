Around the campfire 🔥

Campfire is a tiny, anonymous chat. Up to 4 people share each fire — no accounts, no history, nothing saved. Close the tab and you’re gone for good.

  • You’re handed a random name when you arrive.
  • Links, emails & phone numbers turn to ash — keep it off-grid.
  • If 3 of 4 vote to extinguish someone (the ✕ by their name), they drift to another fire.
  • The fire brightens as more people gather.
  • ♪ toggles the crackle · 🪵 feeds the server.
